The opportunity
Canva's enterprise inflection point

Canva has achieved what most enterprise companies spend years trying to build: ubiquitous product adoption. With 260M+ monthly active users and 95% Fortune 500 penetration, the consumer flywheel is proven. The challenge now is converting bottom-up adoption into top-down enterprise contracts.

Recent moves confirm the enterprise bet: the EY-Canva Alliance for agentic AI in sales workflows, acquisitions of Cavalry (motion graphics) and MangoAI (AI video), the launch of Canva Business bridging Pro and Enterprise tiers, and the Creative OS positioning with the Canva Design Model. The enterprise narrative is shifting from "easy design" to "AI-powered visual communication platform." Field marketing needs to carry that narrative into every enterprise conversation.
