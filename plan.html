Canva field marketing — plan on a page

The integrated framework applied to Canva's PLG-to-enterprise opportunity. Three strategic levers, four growth pillars, and budget allocation.

Canva's B2B enterprise opportunity is at an inflection point. 260M+ monthly active users, 95% Fortune 500 penetration, $3.5B in revenue — but converting consumer adoption into enterprise pipeline and ARR requires a field marketing engine built for a sales-led motion. This plan builds that engine across three levers:
01 — Sales-aligned field programs
ABM, sponsorships, executive hospitality that generate and accelerate enterprise pipeline
02 — Regional GTM execution
NA, EMEA, APAC activations scaled through a lean global team of four senior marketers
03 — Measurement infrastructure
Full-funnel attribution connecting field activity to pipeline and revenue
Business goals 2026–2027
Pipeline generation
Marketing-sourced pipeline growth from field programs → direct B2B ARR contribution
Sales alignment
Sales satisfaction, % of programs co-designed with regional Sales leadership
Enterprise credibility
Analyst coverage, thought leadership, customer evidence volume
Field marketing ROI
Cost-per-opportunity, event-to-pipeline conversion, attribution operational
Strategic principles
Sales-led, not calendar-led. Every field program starts with a pipeline target and a Sales co-sponsor. Weekly pipeline reviews are the heartbeat.
Earn enterprise credibility differently. Canva's consumer brand is an asset and a liability. Show up with analyst validation, customer evidence, executive rigour.
Invest where GTM is most mature. NA is the revenue engine. EMEA and APAC scale in lockstep with regional sales capacity.
Measure everything, kill what doesn't work. Full-funnel attribution from day one. Cut underperforming programs within one quarter.
