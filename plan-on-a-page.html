The diagnosis

Five strategic gaps field marketing must close.

01 · PLG-to-enterprise chasm

Adoption is world-class. Conversion to deployments involving IT, procurement, and security is where deals stall.

02 · Persona translation

A CMO needs a different story than a CHRO, who needs a different story than a CIO. Generic messaging fails.

03 · Offboarding question

The conversation is not "why Canva?" It is "how do we transition off Adobe and what we have?"

04 · Visual experiences

Standard CXO dinners and beige booths underwhelm the buyer and undermine the brand.

05 · Enterprise identity

Internally behind Education and Consumer. Externally, the market does not yet know Canva has a serious enterprise business.

"Field marketing's job is to bridge that gap by replacing entrenched workflows, contracts, and agency relationships."

How budget is allocated

Six growth pillars.

28%

Generate enterprise pipeline

ABM across 200–300 named accounts, Canva Studios at 8–12 tentpole events, 15–20 immersive executive experiences annually.

20%

Create Raving Fans

The flagship program. Pioneer Network, Champion community, peer prospecting, Reference Engine, storytelling at scale, Customer Awards.

17%

PLG-to-enterprise conversion

Adobe displacement battle cards, migration playbooks by stack, champion enablement, partner co-marketing for stack integration.

17%

Scale regional execution

NA: primary Sales counterpart, co-owned pipeline, bi-weekly reviews. EMEA & APAC: localised playbooks, partner co-marketing tiering.

10%

Build enterprise credibility

Forrester & Gartner content, EY-Canva Alliance, enterprise narrative ownership, analyst relations, PR.

8%

Measurement infrastructure

Multi-touch attribution from event registration to closed revenue. Monthly spend-to-pipeline reviews. Kill underperformers within a quarter.

Top-line KPIs

What we measure.

  • Marketing-sourced enterprise pipeline as % of total
  • PLG-account-to-Enterprise conversion rate
  • Win rate vs. Adobe and incumbents
  • Multi-threaded vs. single-threaded deal mix
  • Cost-per-opportunity by program type
  • Reference-attributed deal velocity acceleration
  • Customer NPS lift among enterprise base
First 90 days

Where I would start.

  • Audit current programs against the persona framework
  • Stand up the offboarding playbook v1 — highest-leverage gap
  • Launch Pioneer Program with 12 strategic customers
  • Get one Canva Studios activation in market
  • Establish multi-touch attribution baseline by persona
  • Build the Sales partnership rhythm — bi-weekly reviews, shared goals

The Canva AI 2.0 moment is the kind of inflection point where field marketing either accelerates the enterprise motion — or misses it entirely.
