02 · The diagnosis
Five gaps field marketing must close.
GAP 01
PLG-to-enterprise chasm

Adoption is solved. Conversion to deployments involving IT, procurement, security is where deals stall.

GAP 02
Persona translation

Generic enterprise messaging does not move buying committees. Each persona needs its own value translation.

GAP 03
The offboarding question

"How do we transition off what we have?" is the question Sales has to answer — and currently can't.

GAP 04
Visual experiences

Canva's brand is its competitive moat. Field is the most underused expression of it.

GAP 05
Enterprise identity

Enterprise has the room to define its own narrative — distinct from Education and Consumer, with its own community and visible momentum.

05 · Top-line KPIs
What we measure.
  • Marketing-sourced enterprise pipeline as % of total
  • PLG-account-to-Enterprise conversion rate
  • Win rate against incumbent creative-suite vendors
  • Multi-threaded vs. single-threaded deal mix
  • Reference-attributed deal velocity acceleration
  • Cost-per-opportunity by program type
  • Customer NPS lift among enterprise base
  • Expansion revenue attributable to advocacy
06 · First 90 days
Listen. Plan. Execute. Three phases, fifteen specific deliverables.
DAYS 1 — 30
Listen & diagnose.
  • Sit with NA, EMEA, APAC Sales leadership — pipeline reviews and deal post-mortems
  • Audit current field programs against the persona framework and compelling-event library
  • Map the customer reference base — who we have, who's missing, where the gaps sit
  • Customer interviews: top 10 enterprises on their Pro → Business → Enterprise journey
  • Set baseline: marketing-sourced pipeline %, win rate vs incumbents, multi-threaded mix
DAYS 31 — 60
Plan & align.
  • Co-design the marketing → sales → partner motion with all three function leads
  • Ship Offboarding Playbook v1 — battle cards, migration playbooks, TCO calculator
  • Pioneer Program: 12 strategic customers selected, executive sponsorship secured
  • Persona-mapped enablement library live for Sales — content packs by persona and event
  • Bi-weekly pipeline council established with regional Sales leadership
DAYS 61 — 90
Execute & prove.
  • First Canva Studios activation in market — measure executive engagement
  • Pioneer Summit delivered — intimate, executive-level, 12 customers
  • Champion Network: first 50 champions identified and activated
  • Reference Engine live — sub-24-hour reference proven on a real deal
  • First QBR with CMO and CRO — kill 2–3 underperforming programs based on attribution

The Canva AI 2.0 moment is the kind of inflection point where field marketing either accelerates the enterprise motion — or misses it entirely.
