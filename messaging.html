02 · Messaging & Positioning

How Canva tells
its story to
enterprise.

A platform proposition, six structural rights, and five personas — the system that lets every field program speak with one voice and to the right person at the right time.

01 · Platform proposition

One statement, persona-agnostic.

The proposition

Canva AI 2.0 is the world's only AI platform purpose-built for visual work at enterprise scale. Powered by the Canva Design Model, one brief becomes a complete, on-brand creative operation across every format and channel. 265 million people already know how to use it.

The proposition lands four claims at once: a category (visual work at enterprise scale), a technical moat (the Canva Design Model), an outcome (a complete creative operation), and an unfair advantage (an installed base of 265M users). Every persona-specific message is a translation of this proposition into their language.

02 · Rights to win

Six structural advantages competitors cannot replicate.

Each right maps to specific personas. Field programs select rights based on which personas they need to move.

Right 1

Design-native AI — the Canva Design Model

The only foundation model purpose-built for design. It understands layout, hierarchy, brand, layers, and structure — producing fully layered, editable output, not flat images. Adobe Firefly generates images. Figma AI assists with editing. Neither understands design as a system.

Maps to: Brand Architect, Force Multiplier

Right 2

265 million users already know how to use it

No training. No change management. No multi-year adoption curve. The workforce already knows Canva. Enterprise deployment is an unlock, not an onboarding project. No competitor has this installed-base advantage.

Maps to: Mandate Carrier, Force Multiplier

Right 3

Brand Intelligence — governance at scale, by default

Every design starts on-brand automatically. Retroactive brand updates across thousands of assets in a single instruction. Not a brand guidelines PDF nobody reads — governance infrastructure embedded in the production layer.

Maps to: Brand Architect, Force Multiplier

Right 4

Owned AI infrastructure — pricing stability

Canva's AI models are proprietary: 7× faster and 30× cheaper than comparable frontier models. Enterprise pricing is not exposed to OpenAI or Google rate changes. Customer data stays inside Canva's infrastructure.

Maps to: Commercial Pragmatist, Risk Closer

Right 5

Connectors — the creative hub of the existing stack

Integrations with Slack, Gmail, Google Drive, Zoom, HubSpot, Notion, Google Calendar. Canva does not replace the enterprise stack — it becomes the creative layer on top of it, pulling context from where work already happens.

Maps to: Mandate Carrier, Force Multiplier

Right 6

Canva Shield — enterprise security without compromise

SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, SSO, SAML, SCIM, data residency, granular admin controls, AI indemnification for 100+ seat customers — at no extra cost. Proprietary models mean customer data does not leave Canva's infrastructure.

Maps to: Risk Closer, Commercial Pragmatist

03 · Personas

The five archetypes — situations, not titles.

A title is just a container. The persona is the situation someone is in, the mandate they are carrying, the fear driving them, and the version of success they are trying to reach. Multiple titles can share a persona.

01 The Force Multiplier "Build me a content engine without new headcount."

Situation. Told to produce 5–10× the content with the same team — or fewer. New headcount is not coming. The answer from above is "use AI." They are not looking for a design tool. They are looking for a production system that multiplies what their existing team can do without sacrificing quality.

Value proposition. Multiply your team's output without growing the team or losing quality. One brief becomes a complete, on-brand campaign across every format.

Key rights to win. Design-native AI (usable output, not slop), Brand Intelligence (consistency at velocity), 265M users (no adoption tax), Connectors (AI inside existing workflows).

Proof. 7,800+ design hours saved annually. Tyla Group: 21,000 design hours saved in six months. Expedia Group: 230 hours saved on a single rebrand. 60% reduction in cost per design.

02 The Brand Architect "Get me brand control back without becoming the bottleneck."

Situation. The brand is being violated thousands of times a week — sales decks off-template, regional teams improvising, social posts the brand team never sees. The traditional answer is to centralise design through a small team, but that creates a queue. They want governance infrastructure, not a gatekeeping role.

Value proposition. Brand consistency by default. Templates, approvals, and brand intelligence that prevent off-brand work without slowing the team down.

Key rights to win. Brand Intelligence (automatic on-brand output), Design-native AI (editable layers, not flat images), Canva Shield (governance with audit logs).

Proof. FedEx: 77% reduction in brand review requests within three months. 16,000 employees on a unified brand platform. Docusign: opened the brand to the entire company without losing control.

03 The Mandate Carrier "Make this org AI-native and give me a win I can point to."

Situation. Did not choose this. The board, the CEO, or the CMO handed them an AI mandate and a timeline. They need a visible, defensible win — fast — that proves the AI investment is working. They are looking for the program that becomes the case study, not the experiment that becomes a footnote.

Value proposition. The fastest, lowest-risk path to AI-native productivity at scale. 265M users means deployment is an unlock, not a project.

Key rights to win. 265M users (instant adoption), Connectors (AI in existing workflows), Design-native AI (usable output, not demos).

Proof. 3rd most-used AI tool globally, 27B+ AI uses on Canva, 95% Fortune 500 adoption, eXp Realty rolled out to 82,000 agents — the first company-wide Enterprise rollout in real estate.

04 The Risk Closer "Get control of the AI tools people are already using before this becomes a crisis."

Situation. Employees are using ungoverned AI tools. Data is going places they cannot see. 82% of marketers bypass IT on creative tooling. They are not against AI — they are against the version of AI that creates a breach, a compliance problem, or a legal exposure.

Value proposition. A governed, IT-managed replacement for fragmented creative shadow IT. Same productivity, full visibility.

Key rights to win. Canva Shield (SOC 2, ISO 27001, SSO, SAML, AI indemnification), Owned AI (data stays in Canva), 265M users (govern what they already use).

Proof. Proprietary models 7× faster and 30× cheaper, FedEx SSO rollout completed in minutes, enterprise admin controls, audit logs in beta.

05 The Commercial Pragmatist "Cut this spend and make the decision defensible."

Situation. Must justify every line item. The creative stack is fragmented and invisible — Adobe seats, agency retainers, freelancer invoices, point tools. The Adobe stack is breaking under price pressure. They need a consolidation story that procurement can defend at the next board meeting.

Value proposition. Consolidate fragmented creative spend into one predictable contract. Up to 60% savings versus Adobe replacement.

Key rights to win. Owned AI (pricing stability), Canva Shield (procurement-ready documentation), 265M users (no training cost).

Proof. Forrester TEI: 414% ROI, $2.5M marketer productivity gain, 149% net dollar retention, up to 60% Adobe replacement savings.
